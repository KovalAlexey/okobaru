BMW 2002: A Classic Icon
#

Review
#

This model was developed by Hasegawa and, according to ScaleMates, was first introduced in 2017. However, the plastic parts feel like they were manufactured in the ‘90s or even earlier. I think that because the clear parts for the windows are molded as a single piece, and the interior “bath” is also made as one part. The quality of the internal recessed lines is poor and barely visible. Additionally, the chrome moldings lack grooves for proper positioning on the body. While the kit isn’t highly detailed initially, the end result looks quite realistic. If you can get the paint job right, the outcome will be superb.

For those seeking maximum realism and detail, resin detail sets are available from USCP, and you can also find videos on YouTube showing how to modify your kit.

Painting
#

For painting, I used Mr.Color 100 Wine Red for the body color over a black Vallejo primer. For the seats, I used Vallejo Model Color: Leather Brown (70.871) and Vallejo Game Color: Elfic Flesh (72.098) for the interior. For the body varnish, I applied Mr.Color GX 100 in 2-3 coats. I also used Tamiya X-11 for chrome details and my favorite acrylic metallic, Vallejo Metal Color: Pale Burnt Metal (77.704), for the engine and exhaust system. All weathering was applied to the bottom of the car, including oil stains, yellow rust, and black panel lines, all from Tamiya. One of my favorite techniques is silver dry brushing over black primer. When the result is satisfying, I cover the detail with semi-gloss varnish. This gives a realistic look to metal parts like the suspension and underside components.

Design
#

The BMW 2002 features a timeless design that has aged gracefully over the years. Its compact size, short overhangs, and clean lines give it a sporty and elegant appearance. The iconic round headlights and kidney grille are unmistakably BMW, adding to its charm.

Performance
#

Under the hood, the BMW 2002 is powered by a range of engines that deliver impressive performance. Whether it’s the 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine or the more powerful turbocharged variant, the BMW 2002 offers a thrilling driving experience. Its nimble handling and responsive steering make it a joy to drive on both city streets and winding roads.

Conclusion
#

The BMW 2002 is more than just a car; it’s a symbol of automotive excellence. Its timeless design, exhilarating performance, and rich legacy make it a true classic. Whether you’re a car enthusiast or simply appreciate fine craftsmanship, the BMW 2002 is a car that deserves your attention.
